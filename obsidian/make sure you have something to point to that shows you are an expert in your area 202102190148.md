> ...make sure you have something to point to that shows you are an expert in your area...

You can always try sweet-talking people into working with you but in the the day, the only guarantee of our skill is your past projects. Make sure you have evidence like that (could also be blog posts, youtube videos, or anything else) available to potential clients.

This is the whole premise of the [[Show Your Work 202010191719]] book.
