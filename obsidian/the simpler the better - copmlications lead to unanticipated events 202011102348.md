[[Antifragile 202011062325]]

> The simpler, the better. Complications lead to multiplicative chains of unanticipated effects.

The simpler your approach the better. This works in many areas of life: Cooking, Business, Writing. The reason is that the simpler the outcome/process the less chance that it will fail. 

However, do not mix simplicity and ease. Things that are simple are rarely easy. In fact, to keep things simple is a challenge.
