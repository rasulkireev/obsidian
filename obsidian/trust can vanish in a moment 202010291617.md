> Trust is key and fragile. It can vanish in a moment. As the 19th Century American President Abraham Lincoln wrote: "If you once forfeit the confidence of your fellow citizens, you can never regain their respect and esteem."

It takes forever to build trust, but only takes one action to lose it. Think well, before you take an action that can ruins years of work.
