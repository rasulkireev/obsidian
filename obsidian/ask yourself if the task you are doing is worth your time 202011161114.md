[[The Magic of Doing $10,000 Per Hour Work 202011161114]]

Try to regularly asses if the work you are doing is worth your time. For example, credit card miles and bonuses. If you are spending hours a week optimizing every little point, is it worth the effort? Sure, you'll get some value, but will it move the needle for you? Is there something more valuable you can be doing?

In other words, [[live life with intention 202010160226]] and examine your choices.
