## Highlights
- your ability to process and manage the information flowing through your life is the biggest bottleneck to whatever you want in life
- information is like light: your ability to sense it, to make sense of it, and to decide what it’s telling you is like the ability to see.

- If you knew exactly what to do, how to do it, and when to do it, wouldn’t you just take one clear action after another until it was finished? Concepts like procrastination, distraction, and indecision are just symptoms of underlying uncertainty.
- The level of uncertainty in the world is skyrocketing by the day.
