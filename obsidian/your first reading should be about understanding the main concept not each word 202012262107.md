[[How to Read a Book 202010131541]]

> In tackling a difficult book for the first time, read it through without ever stopping to look up or ponder the things you do not understand right away.

When reading a hard book for the first time, don't stop to check the meaning of each word. For the first reading fluency is much more important the understanding each words. Understanding the premise and the main arguments of the book is more important. Besides, a lot of words will become clear to you through context.
