> We imitate the habits of three groups in particular: 
> - The close. 
> - The many. 
> - The powerful.

There are three groups of people who have the most of effect on out lives/habits. 
- People who are close to us, like friends, family, colleagues. 
- The masses. Fashion trends, gaming/tech trends move us toward specific choices in life.
- The powerful or the People we admire.
