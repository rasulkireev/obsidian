> Tests show that if people play the game over and over, they learn that it is more profitable to cooperate. Repetition tests trust.

It is always better to cooperate. If you cooperate long enough, you will keep earning (speaking in financial terms). Furthermore, you get to help your partner to earn too. 

Some people don't like to cooperate from the beginning. After a certain amount of tries smart people realize that it makes sense to cooperate in the long term.

Trust is key for cooperation. It takes a lot of time to build that trust. But, [[trust can vanish in a moment 202010291617]]
