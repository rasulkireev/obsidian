> Deliberate practice is the only serious way of becoming better at what we are doing.

Note from Kindle: Approaching everything in life deliberately is key. Otherwise it is just a waste of time. In other words, you should [[live life with intention 202010160226]] to achieve your potential.

Deliberate Practice (DP) is something you should do when you are serious about improving a skill. Without DP you won't achieve high levels. It makes sense; DP just means analyzing your performance and focusing on your strong points, or improving weak points, whatever your strategy is. As long as you are approaching this intentionally.
