If you know for certain that an event will happen, take a moment to think about the outcomes. If there is any value to gain from the event, take some time to think through your actions.

This could be an upcoming argument with a spouse, raise discussion with the boss.

Being prepared runs along with [[live life with intention 202010160226]]. Generally, it is a good idea to make decisions that will make your life better, easier.
