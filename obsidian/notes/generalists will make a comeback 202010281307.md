[[Davincism - A Modern Philosophy For People With Various Interest 202010281258]]

In the times of DaVinci generalists were applauded. After the Industrial Revolution Specialization became the hot trend. We are now productivity machines that are encouraged to become expert in one topic.

I believe generalism will make a come back. With better performing machines and AI algorithms, humans will no longer need to do certain tasks. People will be once more required to apply creativity and multitude of skills to solve new problems.
