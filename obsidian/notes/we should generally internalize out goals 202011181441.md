> When we spend time dealing with things over which we have some but not complete control, we should be careful to internalize our goals. My goal in playing tennis, for example, should be not to win the match but to play the best match possible.

When we are dealing with things that we don't have full control over, which are most things, we should be careful with how we state the goal. The same activity can be described such that it becomes fully under your control. You can't be sure that you will win the tennis match, but you can make sure you play to the best of your ability.
