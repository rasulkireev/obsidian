[[Naval Ravikant]]

> You will get rich by giving society what it wants but does not yet know how to get. At scale.

The truth of making money is easy.  

Create something that people want and need, and sell it. This basic strategy will make you a considerable amount of money. If it happens that people don't know what they want and need yet, this is an opportunity to become insanely rich, because you'll be the only visionary who thought of that thing.

This principle adds another skill to the idea to [[learn to build and sell 202010231938]]. Before learning how to build and how to sell you need to invision what that something is. Whether vision is a skill or a gift needs to be considered.
