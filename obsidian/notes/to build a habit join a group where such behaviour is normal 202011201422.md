> To make your habits even more attractive, you can take this strategy one step further. Join a culture where (1) your desired behavior is the normal behavior and (2) you already have something in common with the group.

Another way of making a habit more attractive and therefore easier to build is to join a community where such behavior is normal, or rather desired. If you have something common with that group to begin with, the process of habit building will be even easier.
