> And get in the habit of asking yourself if a person or tool could do what you're doing 80% as well as you.

It makes a lot of sense to automate some tasks you are doing. This will free up your time for the work that requires you full attention and creativity. The only way to achieve this automation is to regularly asking yourself what tasks can be automated or outsourced. This is a habit you need to build. Once you get in the habit of automating or outsourcing, you will be unstoppable.
