> To get what you want, you have to deserve what you want. The world is not yet a crazy enough place to reward a whole bunch of  undeserving people

These are very famous words spoken by [[Charles Munger]] in one of his speeches. In this short sentence Charlie gives a suggestion for any person with any hobby or interest. If you work hard enough to deserve what you want, eventually you will get it.
