> An army of robots is freely available - it's just packed in data centers for heat and space efficiency. Use it.

One of the key factors of success of bootstrapped small business is automation. You need to spend as much time as possible on creative tasks that will make you business stand out. In order to get this time you need to offload as many tasks as possible. 

Thanks to tools like [[Zapier]] and [[IFTTT]], it is possible, you just need to get familiar with those and get in the mindset of automating tasks.
